Be The Change

My Hackathon team and I submitted this project as an entry for the Cypher VII Hackathon in 2022. As such, the site was not fully finished, but it was intended to provide the contact information of survivors to those currently fighting cancer. We hoped that the site would create a network of support for those with similar experiences. This was my first exposure to web design, and I worked primarily on front-end development: specifically, I focused on ensuring that the navbar made smooth transitions between resolution sizes.
